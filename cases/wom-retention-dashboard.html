The Context: "The Portability War"

In 2021-2022, the Chilean telecommunications market experienced its most aggressive period. Customers were churning between carriers rapidly (Port-Out), hunting for better data plans (GB) and devices.

As a key member of the Retention Marketing Team, my mission was to deliver a weekly diagnostic report that enabled the CEO and Directors to understand exactly:
Who is leaving? Where are they going (Entel/Claro/Movistar)? And which retention campaigns are actually working?

The Artifact: Diagnostic Dashboard

This report consolidated hard data from BigQuery (Traffic, Port-Outs) with qualitative NPS (Net Promoter Score) data received via disparate sources, creating a unified "Customer Health" view.

Methodology & Analysis

BigQuery + SQL

Extraction of the "Port-Out" base (customers requesting to leave) joined with billing tables to determine the lost ARPU (Average Revenue Per User) and LTV.

Manual Integration

NPS results and "Reasons for Leaving" arrived in flat files via email. My role was to normalize this qualitative data and blend it with the hard numbers.

To analyze the effectiveness of retention campaigns (e.g., "50% Discount Offer"), I used SQL logic to measure the Control Group vs. Impacted Group:

-- Retention Campaign Effectiveness Analysis (Simplified)
SELECT 
    campaign_name,
    segment,
    COUNT(DISTINCT customer_id) AS total_impacted,
    COUNTIF(status = 'ACTIVE' AND days_since_offer > 30) AS retained_30d,
    ROUND(COUNTIF(status = 'ACTIVE' AND days_since_offer > 30) / COUNT(*) * 100, 2) AS retention_rate
FROM `wom-data-lake.marketing.campaign_results`
WHERE campaign_date BETWEEN '2021-12-01' AND '2021-12-07'
GROUP BY 1, 2
ORDER BY retention_rate DESC;

Modernization Vision (2026)

While this process served its strategic purpose, it relied on manual slide updates (PPT). Today, I would transform this workflow into an automated data product:

  • Ingestion: Connect Qualtrics/Medallia (NPS) APIs directly to BigQuery to eliminate manual emails.
  • Visualization: Replace the weekly PPT with a Looker or Tableau dashboard with daily refresh.
  • Action: Set up Slack alerts if "Port-Out to Entel" spikes by >5% in a single day.

This case demonstrates the ability to handle sensitive business metrics and present them to C-Level executives.
