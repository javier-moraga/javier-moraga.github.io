The Challenge: High-Stakes Coordination

In 2016, C-Level decision-making relied on a unified weekly report. This document wasn't just a spreadsheet; it was the company's compass. My role was to centralize information from Finance, Engineering, and Commercial departments into a coherent narrative, updated weekly with strict deadlines.

Updating the Master Model. Integrating qualitative feedback.
4
15:00 PM
Delivery
Final submission to the General Manager for the Board Meeting.
